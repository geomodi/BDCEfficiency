Hello, my name is Alex Castro. Over the past 18 months, I have immersed myself in the Automotive BDC industry, starting as an agent and quickly recognizing the multitude of challenges posed by various CRMs within a BDC environment. Let's be honest, eLeads and VinSolutions are robust CRMs, but they are designed for single-screen use, typically suited for one BDC agent per dealership. However, the Automotive BDC is a thriving and lucrative business.

In today's BDC landscape, you'll often hear about the effectiveness of scripts and the prowess of agents on the phone. But behind the scenes, agents struggle with slow computers, juggling 10 to 20 different browser windows, and navigating multiple CRMs. Witnessing these inefficiencies firsthand, I felt compelled to make a change.

Drawing from my experience as an agent, I developed a series of tools aimed at alleviating the repetitive tasks that bog down BDC agents. These tools streamline CRM management, reduce human error, and significantly enhance agent efficiency.
